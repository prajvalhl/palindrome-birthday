About

A palindrome is a word/number which reads the same backward as forward. Enter your birthdate and the app will tell you if your birthdate is a palindrome or not. This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy, yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy, yy-mm-dd. For example, if your birthdate is 15 Aug 1947, then app will check in these formats - 15-08-1947, 08-15-1947, 1947-08-15, 15-08-47, 08-15-47, 47-08-15 and tells you if your birthdate is palindrome or not.
